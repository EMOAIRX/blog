latex练习（习题九）

1、判断下列级数是否收敛，若收敛，请求其和 \[ \sum\limits_{k=1}^{+\infty}\frac{1}{4n^2-1}\\ \sum\limits_{k=1}^{N}\frac{1}{4n^2-1} = \sum_{k=1}^{N}\frac{1}{2n-1}-\frac{1}{2n+1} \]

\[ \sum_{n=1}^{+\infty} \frac{1}{\sqrt{n(n+1)}(\sqrt n+\sqrt{n+1})}\\ 变成\dfrac{1}{\sqrt n}-\frac{1}{\sqrt{n+1}} \]

\[ \sum_{n=1}^{+\infty} \frac{n}{3^n}\\ 收敛，因为\sqrt[n]{\dfrac{n}{3^n}} \to\frac{1}{3}\\ 错位相减 \]

\[ \sum_{n=1}^{+\infty}2^{nx}(x<0) =\sum_{n=1}^{+\infty}(2^{x})^n=\lim_{r\to +\infty}\dfrac{1-(2^x)^{r+1}}{1-(2^x)}-1=\frac{1-1+2^x}{1-2^x} \]

\[ \sum_{n=1}^{+\infty}\left(\dfrac{1}{1+\frac{1}{2}+\cdots+\frac{1}{n}}\right)^{\dfrac{1}{n}}\\ 通过\left(\dfrac{1}{1+\frac{1}{2}+\cdots+\frac{1}{n}}\right)>\left(\dfrac{1}{n}\right)^n \]

\[ \sum_{n=1}^{+\infty}\left(\dfrac{2n^2-3}{2n^2+1}\right)^{n^2}\\ \lim_{n\to \infty}\left(1-\dfrac{1}{n^2/2+1/4}\right)^{(n^2/2+1/4)\times \frac{n^2}{n^2/2+1/4}} = \frac{1}{e^{2}} \]

\[ \sum_{n=1}^{+\infty}\dfrac{1}{n\ ln(1+1/n)}\\ \lim_{n\to \infty}\dfrac{1}{n\ ln(1+1/n)}=1 \]

\[ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}(-1)^n\left(\dfrac{n}{n+1}\right)^n\\ (1-\dfrac{1}{n+1})^{(n+1)\cdot\frac{n}{n+1}}=\frac{1}{e} \]

2、判断下列正向级数敛散性 \[ \sum_{n=1}^{+\infty}\dfrac{n^\alpha}{2^{\beta n}}(\alpha,\beta>0)\\ n\gg1,a_n=\frac{n^{\alpha}}{2^{\beta n}},\dfrac{a_n}{a_{n-1}}=\left(\frac{n}{n-1}\right)^\alpha \times \dfrac{1}{2^\beta} \]

\[ 2.\sum_{n=1}^{+\infty}\dfrac{2+(-1)^n}{n^2}\\ n\gg1,\dfrac{2+(-1)^n}{n^2}<\dfrac{1}{n^{1.5}} \]

\[ 3.\sum_{n=1}^{+\infty}\dfrac{1}{n^{1+\frac{1}{n}}}\\ n\gg 1,n\times n^{1/n} < n\ ln\ n,\dfrac{1}{n^{1+\frac{1}{n}}}>\dfrac{1}{n\ ln\ n} \]

\[ 4.\sum_{n=1}^{+\infty}\dfrac{n^k}{(1+1/n)^{\frac{3n}{2}}}\\ \dfrac{n^k}{(1+1/n)^{\frac{3n}{2}}} \sim \frac{n^k}{e^{3/2}}(???) \]

\[ 5.\sum_{n=1}^{+\infty}\left(\sqrt[n]{n}-1\right)^n\\ \sqrt[n]{\left(\sqrt[n]{n}-1\right)^n}<1 \]

\[ 6.\sum_{n=1}^{+\infty}\left[\dfrac{(1+1/n)^n}{e}\right]^n\\ \ln \ a_n=n\cdot(n\ \ln(1+1/n)-1)=n^2(\frac{1}{n}-\frac{1}{2n^2}+o(\frac{1}{n^2}))-n=-\frac{1}{2}+o(1) \]
